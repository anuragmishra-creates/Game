🎲 How to Play Snake & Ladder

Snake and Ladder is a simple board game traditionally played between 2 or more players. The goal is to race your token from square 1 to 100. On your turn, roll the dice and move forward the number of spaces shown.

🎯 Land on a ladder? You'll instantly climb to the top of it!
🐍 Land on a snake? It'll slide you down to its tail!
First to reach square 100 wins!

🧩 Game Features in This Version

  • 🎨 Board Selection: Choose from multiple beautifully designed boards (not customizable).
  • ⚙️ Game Modes: Every board can be played in three unique modes:
    • ✅ Classic Mode: Snakes pull you down, ladders lift you up — the traditional way!
    • 🌀 Reversed Mode: Snakes help you up from their tails, and ladders trip you down — expect the unexpected!
    • ❓ Mixed Mode: Snakes and ladders randomly change behavior like the weather! You'll get alerts when they switch roles.
  • 🎲 Forced Dice Rolls: You can optionally activate a mode to manually set the dice value for each player. Perfect for playful cheating, testing strategies, or just reliving that childhood dream of always rolling sixes!
  • 💾 Save & Load: Your game progress can be easily saved locally in your browser by using the Save button. Come back later and pick up right where you left off by using the Load button. Both of these buttons are present on the top-right panel in the Settings menu.
